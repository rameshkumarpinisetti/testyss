About Us

Welcome to CGYSS

The prevalence of manual judgment systems in yoga competitions at various levels poses significant challenges in terms of efficiency, accuracy, and transparency. The current process of pen-and-paper scoring, especially in large-scale events, leads to prolonged calculation times, delayed results, and an increased risk of errors. Additionally, the absence of a standardized method for displaying scores further complicates matters, leaving competitors unaware of their performance standings.

To address these issues, there is a clear need for the adoption of modern digital scoring systems. Implementing such systems would streamline the judging process, ensure real-time calculation of results, and provide transparency to participants and spectators alike. By embracing technology, yoga competitions can enhance fairness, accuracy, and overall participant experience, ultimately advancing the integrity and prestige of the sport on both local and international stages.
